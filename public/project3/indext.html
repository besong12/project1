Welcome to my website. I am a Cameroonian and a holder of a Bachelor’s degree in Women’s Studies/Sociology and Anthropology from the University of Buea. Before my graduation, I did an internship with ALVF (Association de Lutte Contre les Violences Faites aux Femmes)Yaoundé, Cameroon. An organization dealing with protection of human rights of Women, and refugees, where I provided free legal assistance to refugees. During this period, I organized several workshops and conferences to raise awareness on violence against women, educating victims on how to use their competent bodies and the proper protection tools against violence; educating victims on their rights; Carried out surveys and research on violence against women; supervised and followed-up victims of violence; trained groups on Gender-Based Violence (GBV); and trained social workers on feminist intervention. Thanks to this experience I became aware of the complexities of their situation.
